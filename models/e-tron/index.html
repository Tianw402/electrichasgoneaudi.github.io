Audi e-tron

Audi e-tron 55 had world premiere at September 17. 2018 and was the first all-electric series production model

Later the Audi e-tron 50 and Audi e-tron 50/55 Sportback was released. The car has been a big success for Audi and in Norway it even became the best selling car model in 2020.

Audi e-tron has a wide range of option and advanced driving technlogy. Below you can dig deep in to the different details about this car.
